Earth Observation

The research group SiPEO from TUM develops explorative algorithms to improve information retrieval from remote sensing data.

Noise reduction is a standard step in Earth Observation data processing. SiPEO use advanced Signal Processing techniques - the foundations of which are taught in this unit - to filter and enhance image resolution.
